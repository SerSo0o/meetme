# Meet Me vision

Meet Me is a mobile-first social discovery app focused on helping people connect in real life with lower pressure and better privacy.

## Product identity
- social discovery first
- not just dating
- city, campus, gym, event, nightlife, public-space use cases

## Core loop
1. user becomes visible with a status
2. user discovers nearby people
3. user likes or receives interest
4. mutual interest creates a match
5. matched users can chat

## Statuses
- Green
- Yellow
- Red

## Privacy promise
Users never publicly reveal exact location.
